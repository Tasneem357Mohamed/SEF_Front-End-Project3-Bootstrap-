Wellness & Recovery

The Importance of Stretching

"Don’t skip the cool-down — it’s just as important as the workout itself! Taking time to stretch after exercising helps your body recover, prevents injury, and reduces muscle soreness so you can stay consistent with your fitness goals. Cooling down allows your heart rate to gradually return to normal, improves blood flow to your muscles, and enhances overall flexibility and mobility. Whether it’s a few gentle stretches or deep breathing exercises, giving your body those extra minutes of care makes a big difference in how you feel and perform in the long run."
